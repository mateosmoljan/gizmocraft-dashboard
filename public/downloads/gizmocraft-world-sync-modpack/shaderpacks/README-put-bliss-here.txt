Put Bliss_v2.1.2_(Chocapic13_Shaders_edit).zip in your Minecraft shaderpacks folder and leave it zipped.

Windows: %APPDATA%\.minecraft\shaderpacks\
macOS: ~/Library/Application Support/minecraft/shaderpacks/
Linux: ~/.minecraft/shaderpacks/

Bliss requires Iris Shaders + Sodium on Fabric, or an OptiFine profile.
